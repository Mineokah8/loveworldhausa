Current Series

Rhapsody Of Realities.

Rhapsody of Realities littafi ne na ibada na yau da kullun wanda ke ba da koyarwa da tunani akan bangaskiyar Kirista da ruhi. LoveWorld Incorporation, wanda aka fi sani da Christ Embassy, ​​ma’aikatar bishara ce da ke Legas, Najeriya, karkashin jagorancin Fasto Chris Oyakhilome ce ta shirya shi. Rhapsody of Realities yawanci ya haɗa da karatun yau da kullun, nassosi, addu'o'i, da koyarwa da nufin taimakon Kiristoci su zurfafa fahimtar Littafi Mai Tsarki da dangantakarsu da Allah. Ya ƙunshi batutuwa daban-daban kamar bangaskiya, addu'a, warkaswa, wadata, da rayuwan rayuwa ta Kirista mai nasara.
